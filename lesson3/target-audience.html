Target Audience

Target Audience Description

The target audience for this website is people living in the area of Preston, Idaho and its neighboring towns. Those who are expected to go to this website most frequently would be people very engaged in town events and news. I think of mothers wanting to take their children places and to be a part of the community. Not only that, the general Preston area population who simply wish to see the weather in the area are in mind, as well.

Scenarios

  1. What is the weather going to be like this weekend for our family picnic?
    • Will display weather for the weekend.
  2. I heard there was going to be a festival in town, I wonder when it is?
    • Will provide a banner or site with the information on said festival.
  3. I wonder what ended up happening to that offroading accident the other day? I hope there's good news.
    • Will provide news on recent accident to read about it.
  4. Where can I direct people to information about the gala we're advertising?
    • Will have a link that can direct people to information about the gala for them to use.
  5. I need to find the address to our local petting zoo?
    • Will provide information on the local petting zoo as well as the address to said zoo.
