Site Plan

Site Description

The purpose of this site is to lay forth the purpose and intention for our weather website. It will describe the purpose, name, audience, etc. This said site will be used as an assignment for the class WDD 230 at BYU-Idaho

Domain Name

The domain name for the site will be prestonidweather.com Preston will be the central focus, with its neighboring towns included. The site name will be Weather of Preston Idaho.

Purpose Statement

The purpose of Weather of Preston Idaho will be to inform the public about Preston, Idaho and its neighboring towns of local events, weather, and news. This will be done through our website, where people may check the weather, events, and news as they see fit.
