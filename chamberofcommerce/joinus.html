Join Us

Becoming a TSN Member

By becoming a member of the Spokane Network, you can join us in our mission to strengthen Spokane's business community. You will be given support in your business and in turn, you will also support other businesses in the wider community. We help support your business by:

  • Providing support and tools to grow your business
  • Giving you a stronger voice in wider policies in the Spokane area
  • Giving you access to a community with the same goals as you

Become a member of the Spokane Network today, and you'll be able to broaden your business horizons and expand your ability to help in the wider community.

Membership Level

Membership Types

Gold Membership ($1000)

Gold Members reap the benefits of all that's available to members of the Spokane Network. Such benefits include but are not limited to:

  • Unique training with professionals in your area of business
  • Direct consulting with the biggest names in your area of business
  • Highest prominence in our directory
  • Luncheon tickets
  • Sponsorships with big business advertising
  • Social Media Advertising
  • Exclusive access to area-wide business conferences
  • Access to the full and entire community of the Spokane Network

Silver Membership ($400)

Silver Members are given access to the majority of all we have to offer for members of the Spokane Network, for less than half the price of Gold. Benefits include but are not limited to:

  • Higher prominence in our directory
  • Luncheon tickets
  • Sponsorships with big business advertising
  • Social Media Advertising
  • Exclusive access to area-wide business conferences
  • Access to the community of the Spokane Network

Bronze Membership ($200)

Bronze Members enjoy the benefits of advertising and participating in the community of the Spokane Network. Benefits include but are not limited to:

  • Luncheon tickets
  • Social Media Advertising
  • Exclusive access to area-wide business conferences
  • Access to the community of the Spokane Network

Non-Profit Memberships ($100)

Non-profit members obtain access to the community of the Spokane Network. Although comparatively small in contribution, non-profit members obtain full access to the community of the Spokane Network and benefits involved with improving Spokane's business community.
