    Things to do in Preston

    Come and visit Preston High School. This high school was where the much of the filming of the popular cult classic Napoleon Dynamite. The high school, including the stage where the famous dance scene took place, is found here. The high school, along with numerous filming locations can be found in Preston.

    The high school is located at 151 E 2nd S, Preston, Idaho.

    Napoleon's house can be found on North Road.

    Pedro's house can be seen at South 2nd East.

    Fans of the movie will be pleased to find all these locations and if they wish, live out the scenes in the movie to their heart's content.
